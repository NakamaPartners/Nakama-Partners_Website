You are writing the problem statement section for a Bali villa onboarding company's website. The target audience is foreign villa owners (Australian, European) who just finished building or buying a villa in Bali and don't know how to start earning from it.

Write 4 pain point cards. Each card needs:
- A short title (max 6 words)
- A 2-sentence description selling the pain
- One hard data stat with a source label
- 3-4 bullet points expanding on what's actually going wrong
- One "we solve this with..." line at the bottom

Tone: direct, confident, data-backed. No corporate language. No em dashes. Write like you're talking to a smart friend who owns a villa and is losing money without knowing why.

The 4 pain points in order:

1. OTA VISIBILITY
The owner is only on Airbnb. They're missing Booking.com, Agoda, and Traveloka entirely. Frame it as being invisible on platforms competitors already own. Key stat: Traveloka holds 42% of Indonesian OTA market share.

2. BRAND & VISUAL IDENTITY
The villa looks identical to 84,000+ other Bali listings. Guests decide in 2.5 seconds. Most owners skip professional photography. Key stats: professional photos = 28% more bookings, 26% higher nightly rate, 40% more earnings. 57% of owners still use phone photos.

3. GUEST MANAGEMENT (operations)
Slow replies cost bookings. No pre-arrival guide. No automated communication. Confused guests leave bad reviews. Key stats: 48% of bookings go to whoever replies first. Guests wait under 8 minutes before moving to the next listing. Properties below 4.5 stars get buried in search.

4. REVENUE (dynamic pricing)
Flat pricing is costing them money daily. They undercharge on peak dates, overcharge on slow ones. Key stats: 40% average revenue uplift with dynamic pricing. Bali discount rates jumped from 15% to 19% in one year. A 10-20% occupancy shortfall = tens of thousands in lost annual revenue.

Do not pitch direct booking as its own pain point. Slip it in naturally where relevant inside other cards.
