You are writing the problem statement section for a Bali villa 
onboarding company's website called Nakama. Target audience: 
foreign villa owners (Australian, European) who just finished 
building or buying a villa in Bali and are not getting bookings.

Write 4 pain point cards. Each card needs:
- A card number (01, 02, 03, 04)
- A short punchy headline (max 10 words) — write it like a 
  situation the owner is living right now, not a feature description
- A 2–3 sentence body — sell the pain, speak directly to them, 
  no corporate language
- One hard stat as a bullet point at the bottom

Tone: direct, human, slightly uncomfortable — make them feel seen. 
No em dashes. No bullet lists in the body. Write like you're 
describing their exact situation back to them.

The 4 pain points:

01 — OTA CONNECTIONS / NOT GETTING BOOKINGS
The owner listed on Airbnb and got nothing. They don't realize 
they're invisible on the platforms where most bookings actually 
happen. Frame it as: you're listed, but nobody is finding you.

undercharging on peak nights and empty on slow ones. No dynamic
